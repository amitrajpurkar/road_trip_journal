# 🚗 Road Trip 2026 — Summer Holidays
**May 30 – June 17, 2026** (19 days)

---

## Project Purpose
This folder is the central hub for planning, documenting, and remembering our 2026 summer road trip. It covers everything from pre-trip research to day-by-day journal entries and a final travel record we can revisit for years.

---

## Folder Structure

```
rtrip_2026/
│
├── PLANNING/
│   ├── destinations/     → Research docs for each stop (things to do, eat, see)
│   ├── route/            → Route maps, driving distances, fuel/charging stops
│   ├── budget/           → Overall trip budget, spend tracking
│   ├── packing/          → Packing lists per person / per activity
│   └── bookings/         → Accommodation & activity booking summaries
│
├── ITINERARY/            → Day-by-day itinerary plans (can be one master doc or per-leg)
│
├── JOURNAL/
│   ├── daily-entries/    → Written journal entries per day (narrative / reflective)
│   └── notes-and-ideas/  → Spontaneous notes, mid-trip ideas, lists of things to try
│
├── RESOURCES/
│   ├── confirmations/    → Booking confirmation PDFs / screenshots
│   └── maps/             → Downloaded offline maps, route exports
│
└── OUTPUTS/              → Final deliverables — Excel journal, itinerary docs, PDF summaries
```

## How to Use This Project
1. **Before the trip** — fill in PLANNING/ folders with destination research, bookings, and packing lists.
2. **During the trip** — make daily entries in the Excel journal (shared via Google Drive). Drop quick notes into `JOURNAL/notes-and-ideas/`.
3. **After the trip** — write up fuller narrative entries in `JOURNAL/daily-entries/`. Create a final PDF summary in OUTPUTS/.
